# Cosmic Clicker Game - Interaction Design

## Core Game Loop
- **Main Clicker**: Click on a central planet/celestial object to generate cosmic energy
- **Visual Feedback**: Each click creates particle effects and number animations
- **Energy Counter**: Real-time display of total cosmic energy collected
- **Auto-Generation**: Purchase generators that automatically produce energy over time

## Upgrade System
- **Click Power**: Upgrade the amount of energy generated per click
- **Generator Speed**: Increase the production rate of auto-generators
- **Multipliers**: Unlock multipliers that boost all energy generation
- **Visual Upgrades**: Unlock new planet appearances and particle effects

## Achievement System
- **Click Milestones**: Rewards for reaching click counts (100, 500, 1000, etc.)
- **Energy Milestones**: Rewards for total energy collected
- **Generator Milestones**: Rewards for number of generators purchased
- **Time-based**: Rewards for active play time

## Interactive Elements
1. **Main Click Area**: Large clickable planet with hover effects
2. **Upgrade Buttons**: Purchase and upgrade generators
3. **Achievement Panel**: Track progress and claim rewards
4. **Stats Display**: Show clicks per second, total play time, etc.
5. **Reset Button**: Prestige system to reset with permanent bonuses

## Visual Effects
- **Particle System**: Click explosions with cosmic particles
- **Number Animation**: Floating numbers showing energy gained
- **Progress Bars**: Visual representation of upgrade progress
- **Glow Effects**: Pulsing elements and hover states
- **Background Animation**: Subtle starfield movement

## Game Balance
- **Early Game**: Focus on manual clicking and first upgrades
- **Mid Game**: Shift to automated generation and multiple upgrade paths
- **Late Game**: Prestige system and long-term progression goals
